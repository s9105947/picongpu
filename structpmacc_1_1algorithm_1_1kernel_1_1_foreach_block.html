template<typename BlockDim, typename ThreadBlock = BlockDim>
struct pmacc::algorithm::kernel::ForeachBlock< BlockDim, ThreadBlock >

Special foreach algorithm that calls a cuda kernel.

Behaves like kernel::Foreach, except that is doesn't shift the cursors cell by cell, but shifts them to the top left (front) corner cell of their corresponding cuda block. So if BlockDim is 4x4x4 it shifts 64 cursors to (0,0,0), 64 to (4,0,0), 64 to (8,0,0), ...
