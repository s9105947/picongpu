template<typename T_SeedFunctor = seed::Value< 42 >>
struct picongpu::random::seed::ISeed< T_SeedFunctor >

seed generator interface wrapper

Generated seed is equal on all ranks and can be used together with an rank unique seed to initialize a random number generator. Depending of the generator T_SeedFunctor the seed is reproducible or or changed with each program execution.
