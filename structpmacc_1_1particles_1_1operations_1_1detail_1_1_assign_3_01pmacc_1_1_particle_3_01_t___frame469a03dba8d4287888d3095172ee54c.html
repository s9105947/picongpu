struct pmacc::particles::operations::detail::Particle< T_FrameType2, T_ValueTypeSeq2 >>

Assign common attributes of two particle species.

Assigns all attributes in ValueTypeSeq1 that also exist in T_ValueTypeSeq2 from T_FrameType1 to T_FrameType2.

Assign particle attributes.

The common subset of the attribute lists from both particles is used to set the attributes in dest with the corresponding ones from src. The remaining attributes that only exist in dest (UniqueInDestTypeSeq) are simply set to their default values.
