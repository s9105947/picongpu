template<typename T_ElectronSpecies>
struct picongpu::particles::bremsstrahlung::FillScaledSpectrumMap< T_ElectronSpecies >

Creates a ScaledSpectrum instance for a given electron species and stores it in a map<atomic number, ScaledSpectrum> object.

This functor is called from MySimulation::init() to generate lookup tables.
