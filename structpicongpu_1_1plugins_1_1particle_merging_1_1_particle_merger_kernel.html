Detailed Description

template<class T_ParticlesBox>
struct picongpu::plugins::particleMerging::ParticleMergerKernel< T_ParticlesBox >

Implements a particle merging algorithm based on.

Luu, P. T., Tueckmantel, T., & Pukhov, A. (2016). Voronoi particle merging algorithm for PIC codes. Computer Physics Communications, 202, 165-174.

This method handles the merging process on the single-particle level.

It is called in the main loop of the merging algorithm. Depending on the state of the Voronoi cell where the particle belongs to the execution is forked into distinct sub-processes.
