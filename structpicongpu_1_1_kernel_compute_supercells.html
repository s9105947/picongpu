Detailed Description

template<uint32_t T_numWorkers, typename T_BlockDescription>
struct picongpu::KernelComputeSupercells< T_numWorkers, T_BlockDescription >

discretized field-representation of a derived species property

The derived property of all particles from a species is evaluated with the species' shape to the field grid and reduced of all contributing particles.
